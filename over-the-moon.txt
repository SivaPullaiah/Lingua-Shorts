over the moon



Meaning of the word: extremely happy or delighted about something

How to use it:
When I received the call informing me of my promotion, I was over the moon. It's a fantastic opportunity to take on new challenges and contribute more to the team.

I was over the moon when I got the news that I had won the competition. It's a validation of my hard work and dedication, and I'm thrilled to have this chance to showcase my skills on a broader platform.
